About Me

My name is Lloyd Barnes and I have an English Bulldog (Deebo) and an Asian Forest Scorpion (Drake). I started my career as a cable, telephone, and internet technician before becoming a technical support advisor. I grew up learning HVAC and electrical work with my father which gave me the skills to become a wire technician for AT&T. My experience and degree in technology education gave me the qualifications to become a technical support advisor today. I enjoy gaming, music, and like to travel in my free time.
My goal is to land a career in gaming and/or mobile app development. I am also open to other programming roles, willing to work remotely.
